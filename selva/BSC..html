BSC(COMPUTER SCIENCE)


  • A Bachelor of Science in Computer Science (BSc Computer Science) is an undergraduate academic program that focuses on the study of computers, computational systems, and their applications. It typically spans over three to four years, depending on the educational institution and country.
  • The BSc Computer Science program aims to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of both the theoretical principles and practical applications of computer science. Coursework often includes subjects such as programming languages (such as C, C++, Java, Python), data structures and algorithms, computer architecture, operating systems, software engineering, database management systems, networking, artificial intelligence, and machine learning.
  • Through a combination of lectures, laboratory sessions, projects, and internships, students develop skills in problem-solving, algorithmic thinking, programming, software development, and system design. They also learn about emerging technologies and trends in the field of computer science.
